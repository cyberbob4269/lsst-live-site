Privacy Policy

LSST Live

Effective date: 13 June 2026

Home Terms of Service

1. Overview

LSST Live is designed local-first: the desktop App stores alert data on your computer. We minimize collection of personal information. This policy describes what we handle on websites, social platforms, and optional cloud features.

2. Desktop app

  • Alert cache — broker alerts are saved locally (SQLite) on your machine.
  • No account required — the App does not require login to view the map.
  • Optional TTS — if you enable spoken guide, text may be sent to your configured speech API (e.g. x.ai) under that provider’s privacy terms.
  • No telemetry by default — we do not ship usage analytics in the core local stack.

3. This website

These static pages (hosted via GitHub Pages) do not use cookies or trackers. Server logs may be maintained by GitHub as described in GitHub’s privacy statement.

4. Social media (YouTube, X, TikTok)

When you interact with our public channels, those platforms process your data under their privacy policies. If we enable TikTok’s Content Posting API, we store OAuth tokens securely on the operator’s machine to upload videos the channel owner approves — we do not sell follower data.

5. Third-party astronomy feeds

The App connects to public broker APIs and streams (Lasair, ALeRCE, Fink, NASA, MPC, etc.). Those services have their own terms. We do not send your personal identity to brokers when fetching alerts.

6. Children

Content is family-friendly and educational. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children through the App. Social platforms may impose their own age rules for account holders.

7. Data retention

Local alert data persists until you delete the App’s data folder. Social posts remain on each platform until you or we remove them.

8. Your rights

Depending on your region, you may have rights to access or delete personal data we hold. Contact us via @LSST_LAB for requests related to operator-held credentials or channel data.

9. Changes

We may update this policy. The effective date above will change when we do.
