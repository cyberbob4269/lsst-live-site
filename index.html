LSST Live

A free desktop app and social channel that maps real near-Earth objects, comets, and sky transients from public telescope feeds onto a live 3D solar-system map.

Terms of Service Privacy Policy

What we are

LSST Live (also branded Cosmic Pulse in some videos) is citizen-science outreach for astronomy. Content is suitable for families, students, and curious sky-watchers. We do not publish adult or age-restricted material.

How it works

  • Install the free Windows app — data stays on your computer.
  • Public broker feeds (Lasair, ALeRCE, Fink, NASA NEO, MPC, and others) populate a local map.
  • Short videos on YouTube, X, and TikTok explain notable alerts with clear source credits.

Honesty

We visualize and explain alerts from real surveys. We do not claim AI discovered objects. We are not affiliated with the National Science Foundation or the Vera C. Rubin Observatory.
